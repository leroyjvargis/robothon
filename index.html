Preliminary Sessions

Introductory sessions to Robotics and a brainstorming session with experts

An introduction to what robotics is, what its relevance is and the latest in this field will be covered extensively in these sessions handled by Dr Prahlad Vadakkeppatt, and other dignitaries.

Workshop and Robo-Hack

Introduction to basics, design, fabrication, coding

Familiarization of various aspects of robotics, and the chance to create from scratch (or as close to it), a functional robot of your own design. All materials and components required will be supplied, and participants will be given access to the Mechanical Workshop.

Competition

To find the best project created from scratch at Robo-Hack.

By the end of 24 hours, those teams with a completed robot shall advance to the next round to present their product in front of a panel for 10 minutes. The best designers shall be crowned the winners.

Prize:

Uber Hackers: Rs 30,000

Runners Up: Rs 10,000

All India RAS Meet

A national level student congress to network with RAS members

After the workshop and the competition, an All India RAS Meeting shall follow suit. This maiden event attempts to bring together the active members from across the country to collaborate and network.

Robothon 2015

We made dreams come true! That's right. Getting a beginner to think he can build and manueuvre a robot is such a task! But to sit him down and make him build one anyway in 24 hours was cakewalk! Robothon held from 8 - 11 January 2015 at CEC - a robotic hackathon for the aspiring neophytes did justice to the 37 roboticists who crafted 9 working robots from scratch.

Our experts - a dynamic duo also the CEO and CTO of Sous Chef, a kitchen automation company based in Singapore - Aswin Shibu Abraham and Tushar Mohan gave these amateurs a maze and 24 hours of their expertise and voila! You might have to rethink calling our cohort of 37, "amateur" again.

The quickest team of five to solve the maze with their bot took home a whopping 30,000 INR. The runners up took a not-so-bad 10,000 INR.

"To build and demonstrate" - we did.

Speakers

Dignitaries to meet at Robothon.

Mr Prahlad Vadakkeppatt

He is a prominent robotics researcher and founder and General Secretary of FIRA.

Mr Tushar Mohan

He is a Product Design Engineer at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). He has more than 5 years of experience in developing products and research. Mr Mohan has been serving as an advisor to the RAS chapter of College of Engineering, Chengannur since September 2014.

He is is an alumnus of College of Engineering, Chengannur. He was one of the founding members of its IEEE RAS chapter, and served as its Chairman during the academic year of 2012-2013.
